# Content Pillars & Shows

Four pillars carry everything KurimaSense posts. Each pillar has recurring
**shows** — named, repeatable formats the audience learns to recognise and
look for. Think in episodes, not isolated posts.

Value-first mix (law): **80% teach with no product mention · 20% product.**
Track it on the week board — no week ships more than 20% product content.

---

## Pillar 1 — Field Intelligence *(the core teach)*
What satellite + agronomy can see that the eye can't, and how to act on it.
Stress signals, the KurimaScore logic, scouting smarter, timing decisions.
- **Show: The Playbook** — a numbered, do-this-now method (carousel).
  *e.g. "Three checks before you spray."*
- **Show: The Edge** — one thing evidence sees before you do (insight/hero).
  *e.g. "Canopy cooling lag."*

## Pillar 2 — The Economics *(stakes & proof)*
The money case for not farming blind: losses, input efficiency, margins,
market timing. This is where the **data bank** does the heavy lifting.
- **Show: By The Numbers** — one sourced statistic, unpacked (case-study).
  *e.g. "~20% of cereals lost after harvest (FAO)."*
- **Show: The Proof** — a worked before/after or scenario (comparison/case-study).
  Never a fabricated client — modelled or sourced only (see compliance).

## Pillar 3 — The Ground Truth *(myths, seasons, local reality)*
Zimbabwe-specific husbandry, myth-busting, seasonal timing, climate risk.
Where credibility with the local grower is won or lost.
- **Show: Myth vs Method** — a common belief tested against evidence (comparison).
  *e.g. "More fertiliser = more yield?"*
- **Show: Season Watch** — timely, calendar-anchored guidance (insight/hero).
  *e.g. "First rains: what to confirm before you commit seed."*

## Pillar 4 — The Operator Lens *(product, shown as method)*
The 20%. KurimaSense features shown as *how a sharp operator decides* — never
a feature dump. Product appears as the natural tool for a job already taught.
- **Show: Operator Lens** — one decision, one number, one action (feature/icon-row).
  *e.g. "One number per field: the KurimaScore."*

## The six named shows (quick reference)
| Show | Pillar | Template default | Job |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Playbook | Field Intelligence | listicle / carousel | teach a method |
| The Edge | Field Intelligence | insight / hero | one sharp insight |
| By The Numbers | The Economics | case-study | one sourced stat |
| The Proof | The Economics | comparison / case-study | show the delta |
| Myth vs Method | Ground Truth | comparison | bust a belief |
| Season Watch | Ground Truth | insight / hero | timely guidance |
| Operator Lens | Operator Lens | feature / icon-row | product-as-method |

Shows rotate across the week so the feed reads as a magazine with regular
columns, not a stream of one-offs. A viewer should be able to say "ah, another
By The Numbers" — recognition compounds into trust.

## Mapping to briefs
Every `briefs/<id>.json` names its `pillar` and `show`. The show implies the
default template and the arc (see `carousel-arc.md`); the pillar implies whether
product may appear (only Operator Lens leads with it).
